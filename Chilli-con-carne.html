Chili Con Carne

Description

Meaning "chili with meat",it s a Texan spicy stew containing chili peppers (sometimes in the form of chili powder), meat (usually beef), tomatoes, and often pinto beans or kidney ...

I never been to Texas but this is my interpretation of this dish.

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef 85%
  • Concentrate tomato paste
  • Cayenne and jalapenos chili Cayene cut on the lenght, jalapenos sliced
  • Cumin
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocados Mash
  • Onion and garlic Diced -for the chili and fine for guacamole
  • Kidney and black beans
  • Coriander Chopped
  • Beef stock
  • Tomato Diced for Guacamole
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice

Way to do

The chili

  1. Cook the garlic and the onions
  2. Add the meat
  3. Pre season, it will evolve!
  4. Once cooked add the paste and the stock
  5. Add the beans, the cayenne, and a lot of cumin dont be affraid
  6. Let it reduce untill nice consistency
  7. Check the taste

Guacamole

  1. Mix the diced onion garlic and the avocados
  2. Add the tomatoes
  3. Add the oil and the lemon juice
  4. Season with cumin, salt and pepper
Serve with a full spoon of sour cream on top and chopped coriander
