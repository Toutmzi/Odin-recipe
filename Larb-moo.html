Laab moo

Description

Thai pork salad,fresh.

Ingredients

  • Mince pork
  • Scallots Fined chopped
  • Ginger Peeled and fined dice
  • Lemon grass Fined chopped
  • Garlic Fined diced
  • Spring onion Chopped
  • Mint and coriander Picked
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime juice
  • ChiliChopped

Way to do

  1. Cook the meat, the scallots, the garlic, the ginger and the chili.
  2. Diglaze with fish sauce dont be scared and lime juice.
  3. Add the herbs and the spring onions.
