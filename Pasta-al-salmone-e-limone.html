Nice Fresh original pasta

Description

Stoled From Lorenzo, it's the easiest and fast recipe and so rewarding!

Lorenzo called-it:"Pasta bianca al Limone" This is my verion of it.

Fresh, easy,few ingredients and original, it's a Banger

Ingredients

  • Salmon +-250gr of Fresh Salmon
  • Culinary cream +-300ml
  • Edamame One hand full/two persons
  • Lemon,Orange,Lime Take zest and juice
  • Minthe/chives/coriander Nicely chopped
  • Pasta
  • Onion & garlic Chopped

Way to do

  1. Cook the salmon and take out.
  2. Cook the garlic and onion in the same pan
  3. Add cream and let it cook with the zest and juice
  4. Once the cream thikken add the coocked pasta and the fish and the Edamame
  5. Season, add the herbs
  6. Bon appetit!
