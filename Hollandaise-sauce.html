Hollandaise Sauce

Description

Eaasy to make, dont cook the eggs, dont split go slowly

Ingredients

  • Clarified butter
  • Egg yolk
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Mustard
  • Cayenne pepper

Way to do

  1. Wisk the yolk over bain marie or flame add water lemon juice and vinaigre
  2. Dont stop and look if split add water
  3. Salt, peppers, cayenne
