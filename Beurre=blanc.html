Beurre blanc

Description

Butter sauce with wine reduction.

Ingredients

  • Clarified butter
  • White wine
  • Schallots Choppedtou
  • Black pepper corn
  • Bay leaves

Way to do

  1. Roast the scallots
  2. Add the wine, Bay leave and few pepper corn
  3. Reduce 1/3
  4. Filter
  5. Incorporate the butter slowly to avoid split add water or lemon juice
