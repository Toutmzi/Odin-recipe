Gyozas

Description

Asian Dumplings

Ingredients filliing

  • Minced pork/chicken
  • Onion and garlic Fined diced
  • Red capsicum Fined diced
  • Spring onion Fined chopped
  • Coriander Fined chopped
  • Minth Fined chopped
  • Green apple Fined diced

Dough

  • Rice flour 50%
  • wheat flour 50%

Way to do

  1. Mix all the filling together
  2. Make de dough let it rest one hour une towel
  3. Flat the dough as much as you can and roll into gyozas shape
  4. First you fried in th pan
  5. Add water cover untill cook
  6. Take the lid out untill crispy
Goes well with the creamy soy wasabi
