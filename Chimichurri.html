Chimichurri

Description

Argentinian garlic sauce, goes well with meat and veggies.

Ingredients

  • Vege oil Not olive oil
  • Garlic Fined diced and put a lot dont be scared!
  • Capsicum or chili Fined diced
  • Parsley Fined chopped
  • Coriander Fined chopped
  • lemon Juice and zest

Way to do

Mix all together and season salt and pepper.
