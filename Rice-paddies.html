Rice Paddies

Description

Rice balls fried with creamy wasabi soy sauce

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Onion & garlic Fined diced
  • Red capsicum Fined diced
  • Coriander Fined chopped
  • Minced Chicken/pork
  • Lemon grass Fined chopped dont be scared
  • Mayonnaise
  • Soy Sauce
  • Wasabi

Way to do

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in nice little paddies (wet your hand to make them not sticky)
  2. Mix Mayo, soy sauce and wasabi together
  3. Fried all the paddies untill the meat is cooked
  4. Bon appetit!
